How the App Works:

The app uses the phone's sensors and machine learning to detect potential threats. When a threat is identified, the phone’s vibration motor activates to alert the user. The vibration patterns intuitively convey the threat’s proximity by adjusting intensity and spacing between pulses — stronger, more frequent vibrations indicate closer hazards. Once signal processing is finalized, the machine learning and audio processing framework can be integrated into the Guardian system. The Guardian leverages multimodal sensing to enhance detection accuracy and response timing.
