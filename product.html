Guardian

Safety alerts you can feel

How Guardian Works

Our device uses sensors to first identify and locate the approaching potential threat. Then it transmits a Bluetooth signal to all the connected devices, and once identify the threat with confidence, this will then trigger the intuitive haptic vibrations and strongly encourage the user to move to a safer area.

Solution:Our product ses haptic technology—vibrations—to alert users to potential danger when sound or visual warnings may be missed. By delivering alerts through touch, our technology improves safety in high-risk environments such as work zones. The same approach can benefit pedestrians, individuals with disabilities, and anyone navigating busy or noisy spaces. We are developing both a mobile-based prototype and wearable devices that provide intuitive, real-time vibration alerts to enhance situational awareness and reduce risk.
