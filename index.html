Who We Are

SafeVibes delivers real-time safety alerts through intuitive haptic feedback when traditional methods fail. By communicating risk through touch, our technology helps people detect, interpret, and respond to potential threats with greater confidence and control— supporting awareness, calm, and protection in high-risk environments.

Diverse Applications

Who is our technology supposed to help?

Anyone working on the Road & Construction Sites

Directional haptic alerts designed for high‑noise environments where sirens are often drowned out by traffic or heavy machinery

Those with Sensory Limitations

A vibration-first warning system for people who are Deaf, Hard of Hearing, or blind. Haptic cues indicate the direction of approaching emergency vehicles, enhancing situational awareness.

Pedestrians & Urban Commuting

A tactile layer of awareness for pedestrians, cyclists, and commuters navigating busy streets. Haptic cues support safer movement when visual or auditory attention is limited or divided.

Your Personal Safety

Subtle vibration alerts that enhance everyday awareness of nearby emergency vehicles.
